The Slicing Process

How we transform your digital wireframes and ideas into physical, high-resolution masterpieces.

01

1. Design & Sculpt

You share an STL/OBJ file or describe an idea. We evaluate model geometries, overhangs, and printability, making manual optimizations for the physical medium.

02

2. Material & Slice

We select the ideal printing method (FDM filament, SLA liquid resin, or SLS powder) and slice it into thousands of horizontal instruction layers using custom profiles.

03

3. Layered Manufacture

The sliced layers are sent to our calibrated printer array. Models build slowly over hours, fusing resins or melting polymers with precision down to 0.02mm.

04

4. Post-processing

Completed prints are washed in alcohol, UV-cured, and hand-finished. We remove support lattices, sand lines, and apply protective paints or gold coatings.
